About D_coder


DTU Team D_CODER is dedicated in the advancement of software innovation and excellence for the benefits of students. We help realise a student's potential and bridge the gap between them and the industry. We build a platform that propels knowledge and growth of software department and the university as a whole. D_CODER provides a platform for students for developing their programming and soft skills. Main Activities of our society are:


1. Participation in different Hackathons.
2. Group Discussion on Latest Technologies.
3. Guest Lectures.
4. Regular coding sessions.
5. Idea Discussion on online forum.
6. Competitive coding quizzes
